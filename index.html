Welcome

The game focuses on the player controlling a robot that has broken free of its programming, all other robots have been ordered to deactivate It. My goal was to create a game based on the idea of a futuristic cyber city. The target audience is ages 16+ and will appeal to audiences that like the futuristic element.
Set 1000 years in the future, a virus spred through the programming of all the robots, however something has happend causing one of the robots to break free and become self aware. Meanwhile all of the others have been ordered to track and destroy this one.
